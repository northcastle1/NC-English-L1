The Eight Parts of Speech

01. Noun

A noun is the name of a person, place, thing, or idea. Nouns can be singular or plural.

Ex: John, Cairo, Happiness

02. Pronoun

A pronoun is a word used in place of a noun to avoid repetition.

Ex: She, we, they, it, He

03. Verb

A verb expresses action or being. It is the heart of every sentence.

Ex: play, jump, write, is

04. Adjective

An adjective modifies or describes a noun or pronoun.

Ex: pretty, old, big, smart

05. Adverbs

An adverb modifies or describes a verb, an adjective, or another adverb. It often answers how, when, or where.

"He walked slowly."

"The girl is very beautiful."

Structural Connections

06. Prepositions

Prepositions tell you the relationships between other words in a sentence.

Ex: John lives in Cairo.

07. Conjunctions

A conjunction joins words, phrases, or clauses together.

Ex: Sara and Nancy are good friends.

08. Interjections

An interjection is a word used to express emotion. It is often followed by an exclamation point to show strong feeling.
