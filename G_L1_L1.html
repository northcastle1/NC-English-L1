1. The Vowel Flow

In the street, vowels aren't just letters; they are the breath of the word.

  • A - Apple / At
  • E - Egg / End
  • I - Igloo / It
  • O - Octopus / On
  • U - Umbrella / Up

2. The P vs B Battle

This is where most people trip up. It's all about the air.

P (The Pop):

No voice. Put a tissue in front of your mouth. It should move!
"Pat", "Push", "Stop".

B (The Buzz):

Use your throat. Feel the vibration.
"Bat", "Bush", "Rob".
