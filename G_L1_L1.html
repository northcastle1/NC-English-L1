The Secrets of Pronunciation

The Hook: School vs. Street

Forget what your dusty old textbook told you. In school, they teach you to speak like a robot. In the Street, English is alive! It's fast, it's messy, and it's all about rhythm. If you want to sound like a native, you need to stop reading letters and start feeling the vibrations.

Vowels Secret: The Shape Shifters

Short Vowels

Think "Cat", "Bed", "Sit". They are quick, sharp, and punchy. No hanging around!

Long Vowels

Think "Cake", "Sleep", "Light". These vowels take their time. They stretch out like they're on vacation.

The Trap: P vs. B

This is where most students get caught. The difference is just a puff of air!

P is for PARK

Hold a tissue in front of your mouth. Say "Park". The tissue must move! (Unvoiced air).

B is for BARK

Touch your throat. Say "Bark". You should feel a buzz. (Voiced sound).
